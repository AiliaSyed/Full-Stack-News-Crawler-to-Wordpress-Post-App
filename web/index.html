News Extraction App

Extract News from any Website and post it on your own Wordpress side with its dedicated Language

First, give your Wordpress Credentials, the credentials are there for the API to successfully log into your wordpress site and eventually save the post to it. These are not passed on, but deleted after each session again
Please fill in the credentials of your Wordpress Website

Enter your Wordpress Page Address, it has to look something like: https://en.manqoolat.com/xmlrpc.php

Either you enter a link from which you want to extract the News from, or you enter a title with the corresponding content

Enter Content...

Click on the "OPTIONAL: Choose File" button to upload the third picture:

Please check, that the link does not contain something like: "wp-content/uploads", this will be marked as a forbidden link and the picture can not be extracted

Enter the Image address you want to use as an heading...


OPTIONAL: Enter the Image address of the second picture...


OPTIONAL: Enter the Image address of the third picture...

Last, please enter the language in which the message should be translated. Enter the language in the corresponding abbreviation: For example: German - de , English - en, Urdu - ur .....
